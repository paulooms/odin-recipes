Pizza

Description

Pasta is a very tasty food that is even tastier when you make it at home. It is not very difficult and very satisfying to do. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Pasta noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to instructions on the packaging.
  2. Warm up tomato sauce.
  3. Combine sauce and noodles in a pan.
  4. Plate the noodles, sprinkle with cheese.
