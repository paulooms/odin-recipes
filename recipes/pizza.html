Pizza

Description

Pizza is a very tasty food that is even tastier when you make it at home. It is not very difficult and very satisfying to do. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Pizza crust
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Put the tomato sauce on your pizza crust.
  2. Sprinkle cheese evenly on top of the sauce.
  3. Bake on a pizza steel in a very hot oven until it looks done.
