Bread

Description

Bread is a very tasty food that is even tastier when you make it at home. It is not very difficult and very satisfying to do. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Yeast

Instructions

  1. Make a dough by combining all ingredients.
  2. Knead dough on floured surface for five minutes. Set aside and let rise until doubled.
  3. Shape your bread from the dough. Add to proofing basked and proof for an hour.
  4. Add shaped loaf to heated dutch oven. Bake with lid on for 20 minutes in a hot oven, then another 20 without the lid.
